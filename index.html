1. Assemble Your Roster

Begin your journey and expand your collection by opening Summoning Pods obtained through multiple ways and rewards. Collect a vast array of characters—from street-level heroes to cosmic entities—across 6 distinct classes (Cosmic, Tech, Mutant, Skill, Science, Mystic) ranging from 1-Star to 6-Star.

2. Train & Customize

Turn your characters into powerhouses! Use Essence to level them up to Level 100 and spend Coins to rankup through 5 tiers, unlocking higher level caps. You can also find or buy legendary items like Infinity Stones and Artifacts (e.g., Mjolnir, Cap's Shield) to permanently boost specific stats.

3. Battles & Economy

Form your ultimate team of three and challenge other players to ranked or friendly duels. The combat system features deep strategy with Class Advantages, a dynamic CP (Charge Point) system for Special Moves, and tactical options like Dodge and Parry. Climb the leaderboard to earn wonderful rewards.
