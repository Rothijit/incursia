PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: 26th November, 2025

1. INTRODUCTION

Incursia ("We", "Us", "Our", or "The Service") is a Discord bot application designed for entertainment, economy, and gaming purposes. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

By inviting, installing, or using Incursia, you agree to the data practices described in this policy.

2. DATA WE COLLECT

To provide our services (such as saving your game progress, inventory, and balance), we collect specific information from your Discord account and your interactions with the bot.

A. Automatically Collected Information

When you interact with the Service, we may collect:

  • Discord User ID: A unique numerical identifier used to associate your save file, inventory, and stats with your account.
  • Discord Username & Discriminator: To display your name on leaderboards, profiles, and game messages.
  • Server (Guild) ID: To manage server-specific settings (e.g., prefixes, spawn channels).
  • Channel ID: To know where to send responses and spawn game events.

B. Game & Usage Data

We generate and store data related to your gameplay, including but not limited to:

  • Virtual Assets: Inventories (cards, characters, items), currency balances, and experience points (EXP).
  • Statistics: Win/loss records, command usage history, and vote streaks.
  • Transaction History: Logs of trades between users (to prevent fraud and enforce our Terms of Service).

C. Message Content

  • Command Processing: We process the text content of messages starting with our prefix (e.g., !, /) to execute commands.
  • No Permanent Storage: We do not permanently store your message content (chat logs) unless you voluntarily submit it via a Feedback or Bug Report command.

3. HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

We use the collected information solely for the operation and improvement of the Service:

  • Core Gameplay: To maintain your persistent save file (e.g., ensuring you don't lose your coins or items when the bot restarts).
  • Service Customization: To remember your preferences (e.g., turned-off notifications, custom prefixes).
  • Security & Enforcement: To detect cheating, macro usage, and "alt-farming" in violation of our Terms of Service.
  • Analytics: To monitor bot performance, server growth, and feature popularity (using aggregated, non-personally identifiable data).

4. DATA SHARING & THIRD PARTIES

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. However, data may be processed by trusted third-party services essential for our operation:

  • Hosting Providers: (e.g., AWS, DigitalOcean, Heroku) where our databases and application code are hosted.
  • Database Services: (e.g., MongoDB, Redis) used to store your game data securely.
  • Vote Aggregators: (e.g., Top.gg) which may receive your User ID to validate vote rewards.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to enforce our Terms of Service (e.g., reporting severe abuse to Discord Trust & Safety).

5. DATA RETENTION

We retain your personal data for as long as is necessary to provide the Service to you.

  • Active Accounts: Your game data is kept indefinitely while you are an active user.
  • Inactive Accounts: We reserve the right to delete data from users who have not interacted with the Service for a significant period (e.g., 365 days) to optimize database performance.
